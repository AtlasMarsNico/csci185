Game Overview

The world is a bright colorful place. Aska, the witch of darkness, despised this. Using a spell, they stripped the color from the world. Iri Nova is a witch who specializes in color. The boy decides to go on an adventure with Arlo, his best friend and familiar, to give color back to the world.

Iri Nova

Iri Nova is a witch who lives in a small village whose focus is on color magic. He is still a young witch but he has a lot of love for the colors of the world. His will shines as bright as the brightest star and as vividly as a rainbow during a sunshower.
